Business Areas

Official service profile

The company’s registered objects provide a broad operational scope. For a public-facing website, these are presented as structured business areas to support clarity and commercial positioning.

1. General Contracting & Merchandising

Business coverage includes general contracting and general merchant activities, together with buying, selling, manufacturing, and dealing in articles, substances, products, systems, and appliances connected to the company’s objectives.

2. Real Estate & Property Development

The company’s objects include real estate, landed property, property management, property development, and the acquisition, holding, improvement, alteration, leasing, sale, or disposal of land and buildings.

3. Agro-Allied Business

Registered scope also covers agro-allied products and multiple forms of farming, including arable farming, poultry, animal husbandry, mechanized farming, organic farming, fish farming, production, food processing, and crop cultivation.

4. Import, Export, Supply & Distribution

The company may engage in import, export, trading, marketing, supplies, sales, warehousing, commission agency, manufacturers’ representation, and distribution of goods and products used in connection with its business operations.

How this is presented publicly
